Simulating the board game

Take it Easy is a popular combinatoric board game. In Germany you can buy it from Ravensburger. This library estimates the probabilities of all possible scores by Wang Landau simulation. It is a configuration space for mocasinns.

What works?

  • Estimation of the complete Take it Easy score space

What's to come?

  • Partially fixed (in-game) configurations -> what are my chances, if I put this tile here or there?
  • Nice graphical output scripts
